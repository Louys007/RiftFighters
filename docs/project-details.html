Rift-Fighters

Rift Fighters is a multiplayer fighting game where characters from different eras battle each other. As in any fighting game, the goal is to deplete the opponent's health bar.

Currently, two character archetypes are implemented:
• Cromagnon: A heavy fighter relying on brute strength.
• Robot: A more agile and technologically advanced fighter.

The game can be played on the same machine, over a local network (LAN), or online (WAN), allowing two players to compete.
